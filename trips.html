What Attracions are available on Tripwe?

Tripwe offers 3 types of adventures: water adventure, land adventure, and sky adventure.
  • Water Adventure: Seadoo Safari, jetski, rafting, etc
  • Land Adventure: offroad, camping, trekking, etc
  • Sky Adventure: paragliding, etc

How do I make a booking?

  • Login to your Tripwe account or if you don't have an account, please sign up first.
  • On Tripwe home page, choose one of the three menus (Water, Land or Sky Adventure.)
  • Browse the menu and choose a package
  • Or you can also search for specific trips or packages on the Search feature or the Search bar on the Tripwe home page.
  • Click Book on the Package Details page.
  • You will be directed to payment and finish the booking.

How do I book a Seadoo Safari trip?

  • Login to a Tripwe account or if you don't have a Tripwe account, please sign up first.
  • On the Tripwe home page, click the Seadoo Safari menu.
  • Specify the dock location, date and number of jet ski units click Search. Or you can use the Search feature on the Tripwe home page.
  • ○ Select the Seadoo Safari package you want from the search results to display Order Details, Total Price and Map information (to see the Seadoo Safari location)
  • ○ On the Seadoo Safari package details page, you can click Book to make an order.

How do I pay for the package I booked and what payment methods are available?

To pay for packages on Tripwe with each payment method, you can see the details here: If you want to pay using TRIPWEPAY, follow these steps:
  1. Complete your order and process it to the payment step.
  2. Choose TripwePay as the payment method
  3. Enter your TripwePay PIN (6 Digits).
  4. If the payment is successful, then you will get a notification on WhatsApp.
If you want to make a payment using WIRE TRANSFER:
  1. Complete your order and process it to the payment step.
  2. Choose Bank Transfer payment method
  3. Information on the payment page will appear such as: Total Payment, Unique Code and Remaining Payment Time.
  4. Make a bank transfer via ATM or M-banking to BNI bank virtual account listed on the payment page. (Don't forget, transfer according to the number of orders that have been added with a unique code.)
  5. If the payment is successful, then you will get a notification on WhatsApp.
If you want to make a payment using CREDIT CARD:
  1. Complete your order and process it to the payment step.
  2. Choose Credit Card as the payment method.
  3. Make sure your email on your profile account is filled.
  4. On the payment page, the Total Payment information will appear
  5. Complete the Name (according to the name on the Credit Card), Card Number, CVV Number and Card Expiration Date.
  6. Click Process Payment.
  7. Follow the payment flow according to your bank.

What should I do if the payment info page has not copied the virtual account number and the nominal amount of the payment?

  • Click Invoice

  • Choose the booking
  • Click Order Details

What do I need to do to confirm my order?

You do not need to confirm the order manually because Tripwe has confirmed it through the system. However, if there is something else that needs to be confirmed, the merchant will contact the user directly.

I have completed the payment, what should I do next?

Come to the meeting location on the date and time listed on the invoice. Then, show the order QR code to the adventure operator on duty.

If my order fails, will my balance be refunded?

If you have paid an order using TripwePay or any other payment methods but failed, your money will be returned to your TripwePay account.

How do I view my order history on Tripwe?

The entire order history is in the invoice section of the menu.

How do I use the voucher?

There are 2 ways, namely:
  1. Click the selected voucher in the My Voucher menu and select the package, then checkout.
  2. On the payment page, enter the voucher code or select the available voucher, then checkout.

Can I use more than one voucher?

For now, 1 transaction can only use 1 voucher.

How do I get a voucher?

Vouchers can be obtained by purchasing vouchers which will be available in the near future on the Tripwe app.

Can the Voucher I get be redeemed?

Vouchers in the Tripwe application cannot be cashed.

How do I find out the stock of the package I want to buy?

The package stock can be found on the booking page, when you want to determine the booking date.

How do I view the vouchers I have?

All active vouchers are in the My Voucher menu.
