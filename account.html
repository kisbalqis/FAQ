How do I sign up for an account?

Tripwe account sign up steps:

  1. Create an account on Tripwe and fill in your Name, Mobile Number and Password then click Sign Up.
  2. Verify the account by entering the OTP* sent to your SMS/WhatsApp and clicking OK
  3. You have successfully signed up and created a Tripwe accoun

*Attention, Trippers! DO NOT SHARE YOUR OTP TO ANYONE. Not even to anyone claiming to be Tripwe official.

If you are unable to sign up for a Tripwe account, make sure the mobile number you are signing up is still active and has never been used on a Tripwe account (1 mobile number can only be used for 1 Tripwe account.)

How do I log in to my account?

Tripwe account login steps:

  1. Enter Mobile Number and Password that you registered on Tripwe
  2. Click ‘Login’

How do I change my profile account?

  1. Click Account on the main page of the Tripwe application
  2. Click Account Settings on the account page
  3. Click My Profile
  4. Make the changes you want to the profile
  5. Click Save

What should I do if I forget my account password?

  1. On the login page click ‘Forgot Password?’
  2. Enter Mobile Number, New Password and Repeat the New Password
  3. Click My Profile
  4. Click Next

How to change passwords?

  1. Click Account on the front page of the Tripwe application
  2. Click Account Settings on the account page
  3. Click My Profile
  4. Click Change Password
  5. Enter Old Password and New Password
  6. Click Save

Are there any restrictions on changing passwords?

There is no limit on changing the Tripwe account password
