1. TOP UP TRIPWEPAY: BNI & NON BNI

How do I see my balance on Tripwepay?

TripwePay balance can be seen on the home page or on the TripwePay menu.

How do I top up my Tripwepay balance?

Before you top up TripwePay balance, make sure that you select the amount first
From BNI

a. Top up via BNI ATM

  1. Insert your ATM card and enter your PIN
  2. Select Other Menu > Transfer > select the type of account “Savings”
  3. Choose Virtual Account Billing and enter your Virtual Account number (example: 988247xxxxxxxxxx)
  4. The bill that must be paid will appear on the confirmation screen
  5. Check the data on screen. Ensure that the Total Amount is correct. If correct, enter transaction password, and the choose Yes
b. Top up via BNI Mobile Banking
  1. Login to BNI Mobile Banking
  2. Select Transfer > Virtual Account Billing
  3. Enter your Virtual Account number (example: 988247xxxxxxxxxx)
  4. The bill that must be paid will appear on the confirmation screen
  5. Check the data on screen. Ensure that the Total Amount is correct. If correct, enter transaction password, and the choose Yes
From other Bank Account

a. Top up via ATM

  1. Insert your ATM Card and enter your PIN
  2. Choose Other Transaction and select Transfer
  3. Choose Other Bank and select BNI or enter BNI bank code (009)
  4. Enter your TripwePay Account number (example: 988247xxxxxxxxxx)
  5. Enter the amount of money you'd like to transfer
  6. Follow the instructions to complete the transaction
b. Top up via m-Banking
  1. Login to m-Banking apps
  2. Choose Transfer > Other Bank and select BNI or enter BNI bank Code (009)
  3. Enter your TripwePay Account number (example: 988247xxxxxxxxxx)
  4. Enter the amount of money you'd like to transfer.
  5. Follow the instructions to complete the transaction

How to view TripwePay transaction history?

TripwePay transaction history can be seen in the TripwePay menu

Can we see TripwePay transactions in the last month?

  1. Open the TripwePay menu
  2. Click the ‘clock’ icon on the right.
  3. Set the start and end date to see the transactions during that period.

How to withdraw TripwePay balance?

For now, your TripwePay balance can only be transferred to your own bank account.

Will I be charged an additional fee when transferring to a bank other than BNI?

Yes. Transaction fees are charged if you withdraw their TripwePay balance to a bank other than BNI. The amount is in accordance with the provisions of the destination bank.

What is the HOLD Amount on my TripwePay account?

The hold amount is the funds held by Tripwe which will be disbursed when the admin completes the package ordered by trippers.

I forgot my TripwePay PIN, what should I do?

Go to the TripwePay menu and enter a new account PIN.
